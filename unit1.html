About Image & Form

Image and Form Games is a Swedish indie game development team, best known for their SteamWorld games, a series where each entry is a different genre of game.

The SteamWorld Games

  1. SteamWorld Tower Defense (2010)
  2. SteamWorld Dig (2013)
  3. SteamWorld Heist (2015)
  4. SteamWorld Dig 2 (2017)
  5. SteamWorld Quest (2019)

Image and Form is part of Thunderful Games, a larger publishing group also based in Sweden.
